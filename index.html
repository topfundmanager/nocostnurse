Once your referred colleague completes their first 40 hours of placement in our network, your bonus ($250-$500 depending on specialty) is processed. We issue payments via direct deposit or professional check within 5 business days of the milestone.

We are looking for high-quality RNs, LPNs, CNAs, and Therapists (OT/PT/RT) who are authorized to work in Georgia. Medicaid approval is a plus, but we can assist qualified candidates with the enrollment process if they meet our clinical standards.

After you submit the questionnaire, our team typically reviews submissions and reaches out within 1-2 business days. The full assessment process may vary depending on the complexity of each case and the responsiveness of the referred individual.

Referral payouts range from $500 to $2,000 and are determined by several factors including the level of care required, the complexity of the case, and the services ultimately provided. Once a referral is accepted into our program, we'll communicate the specific payout amount.
