How It Works

Our referral process is simple and rewarding. Here's what to expect when you connect a family with our services.

1

Fill Out the Questionnaire

Begin by completing our detailed questionnaire. This form is designed to gather essential information about the referral, ensuring we understand their needs and circumstances. Please provide as much detail as possible, as this will assist our staff in making an informed decision.

2

Staff Contact

Once your questionnaire is submitted, our dedicated team will review the information and reach out to the referral you provided. Our staff is trained to communicate effectively and compassionately, ensuring that potential clients feel supported throughout the process.

3

Acceptance of Referral

After our staff speaks with the referral, they will assess the case and determine if it meets our criteria for acceptance. This step is crucial, as we strive to provide services to those who will benefit most from our offerings.

4

Payout Structure

For every referral that is accepted into our program, you will receive a payout ranging from $500 to $2,000. The exact amount of the payout will depend on several factors, including the specifics of each case and the level of support required. Rest assured, our goal is to reward you fairly for helping us connect with individuals who can benefit from our services.

5

Continuous Communication

Throughout the referral process, we will keep you updated on its status. You will receive notifications upon acceptance and any relevant follow-up regarding the referral's journey within our program.

6

Your Impact

By participating in our referral program, you are not only earning a financial reward but also making a significant impact on someone's life. Your referrals help us extend our outreach and provide valuable services to those in need.
